YouOverall Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

YouOverall ("the app") rates your athletic performance against population benchmarks. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and what happens to it. It's written in plain language on purpose.

What we collect

  • Account information: your email address, password (stored securely by our authentication provider, never visible to us), and display name.
  • Profile information: sex, age, height, weight, country, and main sport. These are used to compare your results against the right benchmark group.
  • Athletic test results: the raw results you enter (times, distances, reps, watts, etc.) and the 1–99 scores calculated from them, including your full history of entries over time.
  • Profile photo: if you choose to add one from your photo library.

The app does not collect your precise location, contacts, or any data from your device beyond what you enter.

How we use it

  • Scoring: your age and sex determine which population benchmarks your results are compared against.
  • Leaderboards: your display name, country, overall score, and percentile are visible to other users of the app on the global and country leaderboards.
  • Progress tracking: your test history is stored so you can see how your performance changes over time.
  • Aggregate analysis: we may analyze user performance data in aggregate, anonymized form (for example, average scores by age group or sport) to understand trends and improve the app. This analysis never identifies you individually.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not show ads.

Where your data lives

Your data is stored with Supabase, our cloud database and authentication provider. Your profile photo is stored in cloud storage associated with your account.

Your choices and rights

  • You can view and edit your profile information at any time in the app's Settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data — email us at the address below and we will remove it.
  • You can choose not to upload a profile photo; the app works fully without one.

Children

YouOverall is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the date above.
