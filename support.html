Found a bug?

The fastest way to report a bug is directly in the app: open the menu (☰) and tap Report a bug. Your report goes straight to the developer. You can also email us using the address above.

Account & data

You can edit your profile anytime in the app's Settings, and permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete account. For anything else related to your data, see our Privacy Policy or email us.
